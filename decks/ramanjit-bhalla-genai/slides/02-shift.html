SPARC / GenAI
What changed in the product claim
Before

AI mostly explained the answer after deterministic selection.

The recommendation itself was deterministic. Gemini mainly helped with outreach and explanatory copy, which was useful, but it did not make the core claim “GenAI-driven recommendation” operationally true.

Now

AI participates in ranking, while deterministic logic still owns eligibility.

The engine first computes risk, hard gates, underwriting constraints, and eligible products or bundles. Only then does Gemini rerank the shortlist in strict JSON using startup profile, risk scores, compliance triggers, and constraints.

Shift 01
Shortlist first
No product reaches Gemini unless deterministic logic already says it is eligible for that startup.
Shift 02
Rerank second
GenAI returns ranked recommendations, confidence, rationale, fit explanation, and missing information in machine-checkable form.
Shift 03
Audit always
The response includes mode, source, error state, bundle match, shadow diff, and audit metadata so the RM can see whether AI changed anything.
The claim is now: GenAI influences ranking, not eligibility.
