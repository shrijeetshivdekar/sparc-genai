SPARC — Full Feature Breakdown
HOME SCREEN
1. Auto-Profile Search (AI)
Type any Indian startup name → SPARC pulls live public data via Gemini + Google Search grounding, auto-fills the entire 13-dimension risk profile, runs the full scoring engine, and returns a complete recommendation — no form required.

2. Quick-Try Pills
One-click buttons (Zepto, Razorpay, CRED, Ather Energy, Pristyn Care) that fire the auto-profile instantly.

3. Pipeline Intelligence Dashboard
Curated list of 262+ verified Indian startups ranked by estimated premium opportunity, stage urgency, and sector concentration. Filterable by sector, stage, and market status (Pre-seed / Seed / Strike Now / Covered). Includes a sector heat map view.

4. RM Quick Classify
Shorter input form (~10 fields). Plain-English output. Designed for a fast meeting prep conversation — walk out with a bundle and a pitch.

5. Full SPARC Assessment (Manual Form)
Complete 50+ field intake across all 13 risk dimensions. For when you have detailed information about the company.

INPUTS (Manual Form)
Category	Fields
Identity	Company name, sector, sub-sector, funding stage, team size
Operations	Business model (B2B/B2C %), operations type (Digital / Hybrid / Physical), state footprint
Financial	Annual revenue (Cr), total insurable asset value, gross profit
Data & Cyber	Data types handled (PII, payments, health, etc.), data sensitivity, records count, DPDP status, CERT-In obligations
Regulatory	RBI/SEBI/IRDAI licensed, FSSAI, CDSCO, DGCA, labour codes, MV Act, etc.
Physical	Vehicles/fleet count, vehicle types, warehouses, manufacturing plant, cold chain, drones, retail stores
Governance	Investor type, holdco domicile, founder equity %, independent directors, DPIIT recognition, POSH IC
Specialty	AI tier, healthcare operations, payment/card program, export %, Chinese supplier %, gig headcount %, product recall, food/pharma manufacturing, event operations, claims history
Quote inputs	Sum insured per cover, location count, turnover, contract value
OUTPUT — TAB 1: BUNDLE
6. Recommended Bundle
Named ICICI Lombard insurance bundle matched deterministically to the startup's sector + stage + operating model. Shows mandatory covers, optional covers, and a "why this bundle" explanation personalised by Gemini.

7. Why It Matters (GenAI)
Per-product personalised reason why each cover is relevant to this specific company — not generic copy. Generated by Gemini based on sector, stage, data types, and regulatory triggers.

8. Bundle Alternatives
Up to 2 runner-up bundles with their match scores and why they ranked lower — shown in a collapsible section.

9. Companion Bundle
If the primary bundle has a natural complement (e.g. a cyber add-on for a fintech bundle), it's shown as a paired recommendation with its own cover list.

10. Claims Scenarios
3 realistic claim scenarios for the matched sector — shows what would actually trigger a payout under this bundle (e.g. "Zepto: a data breach affecting 2M users triggers Cyber Liability").

11. Additional Recommended Products
Products recommended by the scoring engine that fall outside the matched bundle — ranked by priority (Critical / High / Medium).

12. Not-Preferred Products
Products explicitly flagged as poor fits for this profile, with the reason why (e.g. "Marine Cargo: no import/export operations detected").

13. Download Founder Summary (PDF)
One-click PDF export of the bundle recommendation, formatted for sharing with the startup founder.

OUTPUT — TAB 2: OUTREACH
14. Pitch Bullets (Deterministic)
3–5 sharp, meeting-ready pitch bullets generated from the risk scores — available instantly without Gemini (e.g. "Your DPDP Act obligations + 500L+ customer records make a Cyber breach your highest financial exposure").

15. Objection Handlers (GenAI)
Gemini-personalised responses to the 3 most likely objections from this type of founder — drawn from a curated pitch/objection library, personalised to sector, stage, and bundle. Loaded lazily when you open this tab.

16. Email Drafts — Outreach (GenAI)
3 role-specific email templates (CEO, CTO, CFO) fully personalised to the company — subject line, body, call to action. Each has a Copy and Send Email button. AI-generated via Gemini; falls back to deterministic templates if Gemini is unavailable.

17. Email Preview Modal
Opens a formatted preview of the email with the ICICI Lombard visual template before sending.

OUTPUT — TAB 3: ESTIMATED QUOTE
18. Why This Bundle Was Recommended (GenAI)
Plain-English narrative explanation of the bundle selection — neuromarketing-style, sector-specific, stage-aware. Includes revenue breakdown analysis and graduation path.

19. Bundle-Only Pricing Quote
Estimated premium for just the recommended bundle covers — uses submitted sum insured values, applies bundle discount, adds GST. Shown as a structured table with per-cover breakdown.

20. Full Portfolio Pricing Quote
Estimated premium across all recommended products (not just the bundle) — itemised by cover, with SI, rate, and gross premium per line.

21. Pricing Scale / Enterprise Flag
If the submitted limits are outside startup benchmark range, the benchmark is suppressed and an underwriter-check flag is shown instead of a misleading number.

22. Benchmark Range (Pre-Quote)
Before sum insured is entered — shows a market benchmark range (min–max in lakhs) based on sector and stage norms. Explicitly labelled "not a quote."

23. Graduation Path
The coverage roadmap: what covers this startup should add at the next funding stage (Seed → Series A → Series B+), displayed as a timeline.

24. Product Comparison Table
Side-by-side comparison of all recommended products — cover name, priority, SI range, typical rate, notes. Collapsible.

25. How Is This Calculated? (Methodology)
Expandable modal explaining the full 21-step pricing methodology — inputs, rate assumptions, discount logic, GST treatment.

OUTPUT — TAB 4: RISK SCORES
26. Overall Risk Score (Gauge)
0–100 composite risk score shown as a radial gauge with colour coding (green/amber/red) and a plain-English label (Low / Moderate / High / Critical).

27. 13-Dimension Score Bars
Individual scores across all 13 risk dimensions — Cyber, Regulatory, Liability, Physical, Financial, IP, People, Supply Chain, etc. — shown as horizontal bars with colour coding.

28. Spider / Radar Chart
Interactive radar chart plotting all 13 dimensions — visualises the risk shape of the company at a glance.

29. Top 3 Risk Driver Cards
The three highest-scoring risk dimensions expanded into cards — each shows the score, what's driving it, and what insurance cover addresses it.

30. Score Breakdown — Multipliers Applied
Collapsible section showing exactly which inputs triggered which multipliers in the scoring engine — full auditability of why the scores landed where they did.

31. Full 21-Step Methodology Panel
Expandable deep-dive into the complete scoring methodology — every step from sector baseline through regulatory multipliers to final composite.

OUTPUT — TAB 5: ACTIONS
32. Regulatory Triggers
List of specific regulatory obligations fired for this company (e.g. "DPDP Act: mandatory breach notification within 72 hours") — each with the relevant act and implication.

33. Non-Insurance Actions
Complementary risk mitigation actions that aren't insurance products — legal, governance, operational (e.g. "Constitute a POSH Internal Committee before Series A").

34. Downstream Opportunities
Cross-sell and upsell opportunities flagged by the engine — products the RM can introduce at the next meeting or funding milestone.

35. Global Products Benchmark
How ICICI Lombard's offering compares to global insurance products available for this sector — flags products that exist globally but not yet in India (innovation whitespace).

36. Product Innovation Opportunities
Custom triggers that identify emerging insurance needs not covered by standard products — specific to this company's profile.

37. Refine Profile Panel
Live sliders and toggles to adjust key profile parameters (revenue, team size, data sensitivity, etc.) and immediately re-run the scoring engine without going back to the form.

38. Assumptions Used
Collapsible list of every assumption the engine made when fields were left blank — full transparency on what was inferred vs. inputted.

ALWAYS-VISIBLE (across all tabs)
39. KPI Strip
6 always-visible summary metrics: Overall Risk, Top Risk, Critical Covers count, Bundle Quote, Premium Range, Risk Clusters analysed.

40. Download Full Report
JSON export of the complete analysis result — all scores, recommendations, quotes, triggers, and GenAI outputs.

41. Edit Inputs
Jump back to the full form with the current profile pre-filled — adjust and re-run without starting from scratch.

42. Back to Home
Returns to the landing screen.
