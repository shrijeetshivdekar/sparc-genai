I checked the current v2 bundle logic. Selection is mainly driven by:

`sector + funding_stage + physical asset band + risk scores + compliance triggers`.

Below are practical input recipes.

**Important Caveat**
`Bharat Sookshma Udyam Suraksha` is eligible for micro physical-asset profiles, but in the current v2 scoring it often loses to `MSME Suraksha Kavach` because MSME covers the same property/liability/labour risks with stronger commercial score. It may appear as an alternative unless scoring/config is adjusted.

Premium Estimation Reference — All 9 Bundles
How the engine works (pricing_engine.py):


premium = max(min_floor, exposure_SI × base_rate × combined_loading)
combined_loading = risk × stage × sector × climate × controls × claims × revenue × records
                   (capped at 4.0×)
Stage loadings: Pre-seed 0.90 · Seed 1.00 · Series A 1.12 · Series B+ 1.28

Bundle discount: 3–4 covers → −5% · 5+ covers → −8% · GST: +18%

Size buckets (premium_estimator.py brackets):

micro = Pre-seed/Seed + team ≤ 25
small = Series A, or Seed + team > 25
growth = Series B+
